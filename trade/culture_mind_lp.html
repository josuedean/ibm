STEP 06
SENSITIVITY ANALYSIS

How does the optimal solution respond to changes in parameters? Shadow prices and ranging analysis help the Mind decide where to invest.

SHADOW PRICE COMPUTATION — Dual System
At optimum, binding constraints: 2x+3y=120 and x+2y=70
Dual variables: λ₁ (fabrication), λ₂ (substrate)
Dual conditions: 2λ₁ + λ₂ = 8 (drone obj coef)
                    3λ₁ + 2λ₂ = 12 (habitat obj coef)
Solving: λ₂ = 8−2λ₁ → 3λ₁+2(8−2λ₁)=12 → λ₁ = −4 ... → λ₁ = 4, λ₂ = 0
Multiply 1st eq×2: 4λ₁+2λ₂=16. Subtract 2nd: λ₁=4. Back-sub: λ₂=8−8=0

▸ SHADOW PRICE: FABRICATION = 4

Each additional fabrication hour is worth +4 utility points. If the Orbital could secure 1 more fabrication hour (121 total), Z would increase to 484. This resource has the highest marginal value — acquiring more fab capacity directly improves the optimal utility at a rate of 4 per hour.

▸ SHADOW PRICE: SUBSTRATE = 0

The substrate constraint is binding at the optimum, yet its shadow price is 0 — a consequence of degeneracy. Adding more substrate would not improve Z unless fabrication capacity also increases simultaneously. Both must be relaxed together to escape the degenerate vertex.

▸ RHS RANGING: FABRICATION

The solution basis remains optimal as long as fabrication hours stay between 60 and ∞ (approximately). Below 60 hours, the problem becomes infeasible given y≥20. Above 120, x can grow. The current range is: the basis changes when fab drops to 3(20)=60 (only habitats fill capacity).

▸ OBJECTIVE RANGING: DRONE UTILITY

Currently drones yield 8 utility. The current optimal basis holds while the ratio 8/12 stays between 1/2 and 3/2 — meaning drone utility can range from 6 to 18 without changing the optimal vertex. Outside this range, a different corner becomes better (e.g., producing only habitats at (0,35)).

Managerial insight: Every additional fabrication hour is worth exactly 4 utility points to the Culture Mind. If the Mind could negotiate with other Orbitals for fabrication time, it should be willing to pay up to 4 utility per hour. Substrate is not the binding constraint in isolation — only fabrication capacity limits further gains.
